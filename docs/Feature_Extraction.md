## Anthroposphere
- Part of the environment, made and modified by humans
- Use in Human activities i.e. living, working, communication, feeding.
- includes: Human habitats, agricultural areas, industrial areas, infrastructure and transport network.

***Anthroposphere objects: buildings, roads, railways, power stations, engineering construction (reservoir, dams, dikes, dumps), etc***

---
# Anthropogenic activities with strong impacts on the environment
- Landscaping, e.g, dumps, reservior dams and dikes
- Construction works, i.e. bridges
- Tunneling
- Mining (oil, gas, salt, ore, ...) Opencast, Underground
- Groundwater extraction
- Nuclear weapons testing, etc.

## Primary function of
